# HiveMind × Ravensbourne — Smart Onboarding
**Team 6 | UX Product Discovery Sprint**

---

## Overview

This repository contains all research, design, and documentation produced by Team 6 (Ravensbourne University) as part of the HiveMind UX Product Discovery Sprint. Our focus area is **Smart Onboarding** — designing a structured, AI-assisted digital experience that helps new employees feel confident, informed, and productive from Day 1.

The project runs across four weekly sprints: Discovery, Information Architecture & Wireframing, High-Fidelity UI & Prototype, and Validation.

---

## Problem Statement

New starters across industries consistently experience the same pain points in their first weeks:

- No clear structure or "what to do next" guidance on Day 1
- Information scattered across multiple disconnected tools
- No single source of truth — outdated documents, conflicting versions
- Not knowing who to ask or who owns what
- Reliance on personal notes and memory, which break down at scale
- Conditional trust in AI tools — used, but only when answers are verifiable

These findings emerged from **6 user research interviews** conducted with working professionals across retail, digital marketing, hospitality, healthcare, and architecture & construction.

---

## Solution

A **Smart Onboarding platform** with five core areas:

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Welcome & Setup | Personalised entry point for the new starter |
| My Onboarding Journey | Structured, role-specific Day 1–30 task flow |
| Guided Next Steps | Progress overview, milestones, and daily priorities |
| Support & Contacts | Directory of who to ask, with role-based ownership |
| Resources | Centralised knowledge hub with version-controlled documents |

---

## Key Hypotheses

| # | Hypothesis |
|---|-----------|
| H1 | Role-specific onboarding reduces overwhelm and confusion on Day 1 |
| H2 | A centralised knowledge hub reduces time spent searching for information |
| H3 | An AI assistant that shows its sources increases employee trust |
| H4 | A dashboard with tasks and deadlines helps employees feel on track |
| H5 | Visible ownership maps help new starters get help faster |
| H6 | Version-controlled documents prevent costly errors from outdated information |

---

## North Star Metric

**Time-to-productivity** — the number of days from start date until a new hire completes their first independently-delivered piece of work.

Target: **≥ 20% reduction** vs. current baseline within the first cohort using the system.
