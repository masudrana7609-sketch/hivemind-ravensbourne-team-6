# Success Metrics — Smart Onboarding

## North Star Metric
**Time-to-productivity** — the number of days from start date until a new hire completes their first meaningful, independently-delivered piece of work.

Target: reduce by **≥ 20%** vs. current baseline within the first cohort using the smart onboarding system.

---

## Primary Metrics

| Metric | Description | Target | Measurement Method |
|--------|-------------|--------|--------------------|
| Time-to-productivity | Days to first meaningful contribution | ↓ 20% vs. baseline | Manager assessment at 30/60/90 days |
| Onboarding task completion rate | % of assigned onboarding tasks completed on time | ≥ 85% within 30 days | Platform analytics |
| New hire satisfaction score (NHSS) | Post-onboarding survey score (1–10) | ≥ 8.0 average | Survey at day 30 |
| 90-day retention rate | % of new hires still employed at 90 days | ≥ 95% | HR records |
| Manager confidence score | Manager rating of new hire readiness (1–5) | ≥ 4.0 at day 60 | Manager survey |

---

## Secondary Metrics

| Metric | Description | Target |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Belonging score | New hire sense of connection to team (1–10 survey) | ≥ 7.5 at day 30 |
| Repetitive support requests | Number of repeated questions raised to HR / manager | ↓ 30% vs. baseline |
| Content engagement rate | % of onboarding content modules opened and completed | ≥ 75% |
| Time spent in onboarding platform | Avg. minutes per day engaged with the system | 15–30 min/day (not under or over) |
| Buddy / peer connection rate | % of new hires who have met their assigned buddy within week 1 | ≥ 90% |

---

## Guardrail Metrics
These must not worsen during the project.

| Metric | Threshold |
|--------|-----------|
| HR team workload | Must not increase by more than 10% |
| Manager time spent on onboarding | Must not increase vs. baseline |
| New hire anxiety / stress indicators | Must not worsen (tracked via survey) |

---

## Measurement Timeline

| Milestone | When | What we measure |
|-----------|------|-----------------|
| Baseline capture | Before launch | Current time-to-productivity, satisfaction, retention |
| Week 1 check-in | Day 7 | Task completion rate, buddy connection rate |
| Month 1 review | Day 30 | NHSS, belonging score, content engagement |
| Month 2 review | Day 60 | Manager confidence score, support requests |
| Month 3 final | Day 90 | Retention, full metric review vs. baseline |

---

## How We Define Success
The smart onboarding system is considered a success if, after the first full cohort:

1. Time-to-productivity improves by ≥ 20%
2. New hire satisfaction score is ≥ 8.0
3. 90-day retention is ≥ 95%
4. No guardrail metrics worsen
